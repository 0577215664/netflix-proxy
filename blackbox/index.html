about

black.box is a plug-and-play VPN policy router.

A customer supplied hardware device is required.

Currently Raspberry Pi 3 is the only supported device.

The service is offered on a rolling subscription basis, with first month free.

Afterwards a monthly subscription fee applies, unless the subscription is cancelled prior to the next billing cycle.

instructions

  • Obtain a compatible device and SD card (4GB is more than enough).
  • Download and install Etcher.
  • Download the Device .img file.
  • Start the writer and select the Device .img file in your downloads folder.
  • Insert your SD card and press [Burn!].
  • Warning! This will irreversibly erase your SD card.
  • Wait until it's finished writing.
  • Safely eject the freshly burnt SD card and insert into your device.
  • Connect your device to the Internet using a spare Ethernet port on your router and power it up using the recommended power adapter.
  • After a few minutes, connect to the new Wi-Fi network called black.box.
  • Visit the API link and email your unique guid and subscription email address to support.
  • You should now be connected to a VPN server in the United States.
  • For issues, please contact support.
